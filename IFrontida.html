CONTROL THE NARRATIVE

Now you have the power to create change with your opinion, with poll book, every opinion matters

Pollbook is a platform for individuals to openly air their views and become part of a community holding similar views and opinions. The goal of poll book Is that at some point in the near future organizations, local governments, etc. would be able to hold elections on the platform


My Role

Beyond design, my role was to create a visual representation of the idea the creators of the platform were trying to portray. I was tasked with the responsibility of designing an experience that would inspire users to air their views and contribute to the Growth of a particular school of thought
