Designing the future,
one product at a time

The Product design process is an intersection, it’s an intersection of business goals and consumer needs. As a designer, succeeding within this intersection would mean being able to carefully balance business goals with consumer needs. Over the course of the past 3 years, I’ve helped businesses design world class products that meet business goals while simultaneously creating value for consumers. I believe that good design is a product of an emphatic understanding of the needs of every party involved, and this has guided my approach to design thinking over the years
